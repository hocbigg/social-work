# Summary

The Social Work curriculum is a **complete education in Social Work** using online materials.

Note: When there are courses or books that don't fit into the curriculum but are otherwise of high quality,
they belong in [extras/courses](extras/courses.md) or [extras/readings](extras/readings.md).

# Curriculum

| Subject | Why study? | Book | Videos |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Introduction to Social Work | Provides an overview of the profession's history, values, and roles in society. | *Introduction to Social Work: An Advocacy-Based Profession* | [An Introduction to Social Work](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/health-sports-psychology/social-care-social-work/an-introduction-social-work/content-section-0) |
| Human Behavior in the Social Environment | Explains human development and behavior in social contexts to inform assessments and interventions. | *Human Behavior and the Social Environment: Shifting Paradigms in Essential Knowledge for Social Work Practice* | [Human Behavioral Biology](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL848F2368C90DDC3D) |
| Social Welfare Policy | Examines policies affecting social services and teaches advocacy for equitable systems. | *Social Welfare Policy: Responding to a Changing World* | [Social Policy for Social Services & Health Practitioners](https://www.classcentral.com/course/social-welfare-policy-18610) |
| Social Work Practice | Develops skills for direct practice with individuals, families, groups, and communities. | *Social Work Methods and Skills: The Essential Foundations of Practice* | [Social Work Practice: Advocating Social Justice and Change](https://www.coursera.org/learn/social-work-practice-advocating-social-justice-and-change) |
| Social Work Research Methods | Teaches methods to evaluate practice and contribute to evidence-based social work. | *[Research Methods for Social Work](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/237131811_Research_Methods_for_Social_Work)* | [Social Work: Research](https://www.edx.org/course/social-work-research) |
| Ethics and Social Justice in Social Work | Guides ethical decision-making and promotes justice-oriented practice. | *Social Work Values and Ethics* | [Code of Ethics Training](https://www.socialworkers.org/About/Ethics/Ethics-Education-and-Resources/Code-of-Ethics-Training) |

## Congratulations

After completing the requirements of the curriculum above,
you will have completed the equivalent of a full bachelor's degree in Social Work.
Congratulations!
